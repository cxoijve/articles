Four People Die in Fishing Boat-Freighter Clash. Four fishermen died and three went missing late Saturday after a Korean fishing boat sank after a collision with a foreign cargo ship near waters off Jeju Island. According to police, the 29-ton fishing boat, called "Daekyung," clashed with the a 3,800-ton freighter from Hong Kong around 10 p.m. on Saturday, which killed four of the nine crew on the small fishing boat.
